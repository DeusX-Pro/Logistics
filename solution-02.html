Heavy Lift & Project Cargo

Expert handling of complex lifting operations with systematic assessment including load test, lift plan, risk analysis, job safety protocols, and method statements for routine, heavy, and critical lifts.

Shipping Support Services

Comprehensive vessel services including ship chandelling, supplies, salvage operations, crew welfare arrangements, third-party ship management, and local port representation for vessel owners.

Road Freight & Haulage

Reliable trucking and delivery services for product distribution and project cargo delivery, ensuring safe and timely transportation across Nigeria with our fleet of well-maintained vehicles.

Offshore Logistics Support

Complete offshore field support including warehousing, transfer to quayside, forklift operations, and comprehensive logistics coordination for offshore operations and field activities.

Customs Clearance & Brokerage

Professional customs clearing and brokerage services with expert handling of export and import documentation, ensuring compliance and smooth clearance processes for your shipments.

Bunker & Fresh Water Supply

Quality bunker supply (AGO, MGO) for inbound and outbound vessels with laboratory-certified fuel quality results, plus fresh water supply to meet all vessel requirements at competitive rates.
