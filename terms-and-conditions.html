Service Scope and Operations

  • Heavy Lift & Project Cargo: All lifting operations require systematic assessment including load test, lift plan, risk analysis, and job safety protocols. Clients must provide accurate cargo specifications including weight, dimensions, and center of gravity.
  • Dangerous Cargo Handling: Transportation of IMDG and IATA classified dangerous goods requires proper documentation, safety data sheets, and compliance with international maritime dangerous goods regulations.
  • Documentation Requirements: Accurate and complete shipping documentation, customs clearance papers, and certificates of origin must be provided by the client for all shipments.
  • Offshore Logistics: Offshore field support services are subject to weather conditions and offshore safety protocols. Schedules may be adjusted for operational safety.

  • Claims Procedure: All claims for loss, damage, or delay must be submitted in writing within 14 days of delivery or expected delivery date, accompanied by supporting documentation including bill of lading, commercial invoice, and photographic evidence where applicable.
  • Safety Standards: We maintain occupational safety, health, and environment protocols in all operations. Clients must comply with our safety requirements during cargo handover and operations.
